About Us

"Children are the future of this country. By investing in children's basic education [and safety], we are contributing to the country's development by creating a literate and self-sufficient society." - Rick Corsino

Who we are

Rides4Kidz is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping kids get safer rides from their school to the comfort of their homes. We provide schools an effortless way to request rides for groups of 2-6 children, making sure that each child is chaperoned by a teacher that both the parents and children trust. With Rides4Kidz, these children will now be able to get a safe ride home through the powerful support of their community.

Our mission

"Congress said back in 1974 that school transportation should be held to the highest level of safety," noted Phyllis F. Agran, M.D., lead author of the American Academy of Pediatrics' policy on school transportation safety. "It is very important that parents, pediatricians, and school districts work together to ensure that all children can get to school safely." Rides4Kidz takes the burden off the parents to find the time and means to get their children home. With a safe ride available at a moment's notice, kids can focus more on their education and be able to spend more time with their parents.

What you can do

Rides4Kids hopes to achieve its dream by building a safer community for young children aspiring to get a good education. Please help by donating and helping the schools listed on our site to achieve their dreams of making their institution safer for their students, and in turn allowing the children to continue to grow and reach their true potential
